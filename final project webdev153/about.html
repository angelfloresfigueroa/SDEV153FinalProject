How Angels Sandwich Shop started....

It all started back in 2015 when a young Angel would always be dissapointed in the sandwiches most restaurants make that dissapoint him, after giving thought and starting to experiment on his own sandwiches he became pleased with the outcome of his results and suddenly grew the desire to start his own buisness and open Angels Sandwich Shop. Ever since the first opening of the first location in South Bend Indiana, the buisness expanded exponentialy and has opened multiple locations in a short period of time. As a buisness we provide the tastiest sandwiches that Angel has created and aim to satisfy our customer needs.

We hope to see you soon at one of our locations!

-Angel
